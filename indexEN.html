ABOUT US

The doors of Royal Pizza were opened on May 1, and we have since the opening been focusing on certain values and continuously maintaining the same standard. We are greatly invested in the quality of our pizzas, grilled dishes and the other various dishes on our menu. This we accomplish by using great fresh ingredients carefully handpicked by our experienced coworker. We have more than 15 years of experience and education in the food industry, and hygiene as well as environmental awareness are taken very seriously in our kitchen. At Royal Pizza, we offer great customer service with an always-smiling staff and quick delivery at fair prices.
Royal Pizza is located in the heart of the city of smiles where the food industry is very big and competitive, which encourages us to always do our very best to satisfy our customers. Our menu consists of most of the classic recipes, with no special surprises except for a select few recipes that we cook from scratch, such as our pizza dough, pasta dishes and homemade burgers. The spices that we use are full of taste and love.
Your opinion contributes greatly to our continuous strive for excellence. We look forward to seeing you.
Best Regards,
Royal Pizza Aarhus.
